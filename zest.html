Zest Iterative Redesign

Team

Tiffany Huang
Ryan Peng
Cynthia Wang


Context

UI/UX class @ Brown University


Duration

3 weeks, ~8 hours/week


Tools

Figma

In this project, our team of designers worked with Zest, a corporate gifting platform, to develop interface changes to accomodate a new shipping option.

The Problem: Lack of support for bulk ordering

While users of Zest's gifting platform were able to choose between shipping gifts to individual addresses and sending as e-gifts, there was no option to deliver all gifts to one address.
